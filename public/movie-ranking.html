My Top 3 Anime all time

One Piece

Shonen Manga created by Oda is very exiciting. This is about a kid who got special powers by eating a devil fruit called "Gomu-Gomu" which made his body rubber. Person who eats these type of Devil fruits tend to lose swimming capabilities as these are curesd by Sea itself.

In his journey, he makes lot of friends and has many adventures and life-death expericences.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Koyoharu Gotouge. A kid's family is killed by one Demon who is the boss or final villan called "Muzan", this kid's sister becomes a Demon and he starts searching for Muzan in order to kill him and make his sister back to normal human being.

In his journey, he makes lot of friends and also join Demon Corps which specializes in killing Demons.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a story based on Manga series where our main character eats fingers of "King of Curses" called "Sakura" As per the elders in this manga, he should be killed but "Satoru Gojo" takes this kid into a special high school which teaches him to control the power of curse.
